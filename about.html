About Us

Get to know LIMA, and what our main focus is

LIMA Profile

Founded on May 15, 2012, LIMA is a purpose-driven company committed to empowering Indonesia's university students through an integrated platform built on sports, entertainment, and education. As a leading force in campus life, LIMA bridges academic and non-academic experiences, fostering talent development, creativity, and productivity. We are dedicated to shaping a future generation that is dynamic, impactful, and ready to lead.

Vision

To become the leading platform for student sports competitions
and entertainment — shaping campus culture across Indonesia.

Mission

To build a healthy and sustainable business ecosystem. To continuously innovate student programs across sports, education, and entertainment. To provide a platform for talented and driven university students to grow and thrive.
